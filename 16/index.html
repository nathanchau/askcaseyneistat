I don't sleep

07/14/15 / Vlog #112 / link

With special guests Candice and Francine.

Editors note: This was a short Q and A because Casey forgot about it until late in the day. The vlog instead focused on the history of Beme. At the beginning of this story, Casey provided this insight:

The only thing in life that stands between you and everything you’ve ever wanted to do, is doing it.

How do you find time to vlog and run a business?

I don’t sleep.

Can you do a wheelie on your bicycle or vespa?

Bicycle yes, vespa no.

I’m a 17 year old filmmaker, how do I get started in advertising?

You have to make really great work and then they will come to you. I mean that.

What’s your diet like?

(Candice) Junk food or healthy, nothing in between.

What’s the best part about living in New York City?

(Candice) 24/7.

Do you get irritated when young viewers stop you in the street?

No, it’s always nice.

How big do you see Beme becoming?

I don’t know.

What was the first though that came into your mind when Frannie was born?

Sheer terror. Having a kid is the scariest thing you will ever do.

Will you ever retire?

I’ve been asked that question before. Retirement is something that I have no interest in. I love work so much, work is life. What would you do if you retired?
